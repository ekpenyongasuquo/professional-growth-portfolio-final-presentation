Spiritual Progression Portfolio

Throughout this course, I experienced meaningful spiritual refinement through intentional reflection on my values, decision-making, and reliance on Christ. Professional Growth Portfolio

Professional Development Reflection

This course clarified the importance of ethical responsibility in information technology. I gained a deeper appreciation for how technical competence must be guided by integrity, accountability, and respect for others, especially when handling sensitive data and system access.

Technical Skills and Ethical Application

I engaged with industry-relevant concepts related to data handling, system monitoring, and privacy. This ethical approach prepares me to responsibly manage user data in real-world IT environments where trust, compliance, and confidentiality are critical. I now approach technology as a stewardship responsibility rather than merely a technical challenge.

Career Vision

  • Develop technical competencies aligned with industry standards
  • Uphold ethical practices in data privacy and cybersecurity
  • Apply integrity and accountability in professional decision-making
  • Create technology solutions that positively impact others

Detailed Action Plan for Ongoing Development

  • Set quarterly goals for spiritual reflection and professional growth
  • Participate in ethical training and professional development opportunities
  • Seek mentorship and accountability
  • Evaluate decisions using spiritual and ethical frameworks

Reflective Essay: Alignment and Evolution

My initial goals focused primarily on technical skills and academic success. Over time, these goals evolved to emphasize character, ethical responsibility, and service. This portfolio reflects a holistic vision that integrates faith, professionalism, and lifelong discipleship.
